Our Mission

Madadgar Dastak aims to reduce poverty in Lahore by connecting underprivileged individuals with free essential services provided by NGOs, government programs, and generous donors.

Available Services

Food Assistance

Find free meals, ration distribution centers, and food banks across Lahore.

Housing Support

Shelters and temporary housing options for homeless individuals and families.

Healthcare

Free medical camps, clinics, and hospitals offering treatment to those in need.

Education

Free schools, vocational training, and literacy programs for children and adults.
